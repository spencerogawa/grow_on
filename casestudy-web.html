About

Grow ON is a movement to revitalize the farming demographic of Ontario for future sustainability of the industry and environment.

Our mission is to rejuvenate and reinvigorate the Organic Farming Industry of Ontario through awareness, networking and education. By raising awareness of the current agricultural struggles and promoting the prosperous opportunity, we wish to educate young adults on how to start their own farming business while leveraging a vast cooperative network of resources.

The design strategy contains three phases. First one is to raise awareness/evoke — using merchandise to reach out target audience and gain attention. Secondly, a website to educate them and lastly a mobile app to help them pursue an agricultural career.

Objective

For the second stage, the objective for web design project is to create a well designed website that can appeal to our target audience those who are in their 20s and 30s. The website should be pleasing to view and inform why we need more farmers in Ontario and inspire them to join the movement.

Solution

The solution is to use smooth transition/ movement while navigating through the website by scrolling. Background colour change add more depth to the experience of visiting this website and the use of infographics to convey information with little text. We all know that no one would want to read long paragraphs.

Animated texts to evoke and gain attention from viewers. We used java script for animation rather than just a motion gif so that it would work great for SEO.

Background colour changes as you scroll down to make learning experience more fun.

After going through home page and learn about facts about farming in Ontario, viewer can move to either fundamentals to gain little further information and tips about agricultural business.

Those products also can be purchased online.
